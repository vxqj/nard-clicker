Nard's Maze

he doesn't want you to leave
Somebody dragged you into a sprawling, lightless maze built from Nard's worst memories. It is enormous — finding the way out will take a while. Something wearing his face lives in here now, and it isn't friendly.

• Move with WASD or the Arrow Keys
• Move your mouse to look around, first-person style
• Press F to toggle your flashlight — it helps you see, but it also helps him see you
• Press M to check the full map — the exit is never marked on it
• Collect all 5 polaroids hidden in the maze — each one is XP
• Find the gate switch to unlock the exit
• Reach the exit before he reaches you
• In a party? You'll see your teammates — and their nametags — moving through the same maze
If he catches you, it's over.
